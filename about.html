We exist to make autonomous AI systems trustworthy, interpretable, and useful in the real world — not as demos, but as infrastructure.

Founded by researchers and systems engineers who shipped the previous generation of AI products and now want to build the next one properly.

01 · Mission

Why Dolphin.

Most AI is a conversation. The next decade is about action — agents that operate continuously, safely, and verifiably in real environments.

The current generation of AI products has shown what models can do. The next generation must show what systems can do — assembling perception, planning, memory, tools, and feedback into something that functions reliably without supervision.

That's our entire focus. We don't train frontier models. We build the architecture around them so they become useful: memory that doesn't drift, plans that don't collapse, tools that don't leak, and feedback loops that actually close.

02 · History

Trajectory.

A short timeline of the work so far.

2024 · Q2

Founded

The team forms around a shared thesis: autonomy is an architecture problem, not just a model problem.

2024 · Q4

First runtime

Initial closed-loop agent runtime ships internally. Sustained operation over 72 hours on industrial workflows.

2025 · Q2

Tool layer v1

Unified tool runtime with sandboxing, structured I/O, and policy gating. 100+ integrations.

2025 · Q4

Multi-agent orchestration

First production deployments running coordinated agent fleets across enterprise and robotics customers.

2026 · Now

Opening cohort

Partnering with a small group of teams deploying autonomous agents in production.
